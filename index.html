Paul R. Brown, DPM

Diplomate of the American Board of Podriatric Surgery

Since opening his practice in 1985, Dr. Brown has treated countless patients throughout the Morris County area.  He is a Diplomate of the American Board of Podiatric Surgery, an attending staff member at Morristown Medical Center, and a long-time staff member at the Morristown Wound Healing Center. In his spare time, Dr. Brown can be found at the helm of his Boston Whaler on Barnegat Bay.
